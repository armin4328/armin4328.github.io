HOW TO PLAY!

This is a turn based fighting game, you begin with choosing your characters at the top, left side for player 1, right side for player 2. All characters have their own unique 4 moves, hovering over a move shows what it does, use your character the best that you can to win!

BLEED: Bleed is a debuff applied through moves, bleed will happen every round until the specified amount of rounds is over, bleed will also go through GUARD and can not be stopped.

MARK: Mark is a debuff applied through moves, mark is used in combination with other moves as some moves have special effects IF the player is marked.

RIPOSTE: Riposte is a buff that is applied to yourself, this buff will cause you to reflect 50% of the opponents damage on there turn.

GUARD: Guard is a buff applied to yourself which will reduce incoming damage by 20.
